Game Rules

Welcome to DRISHYAMM! The objective is to capture the hidden VIP piece of the opponent.

1. Players take turns to move pieces according to normal chess rules.
2. Select a VIP piece for each color secretly before the game starts.
3. Capture the opponent's VIP piece to win the game.
4. Standard chess piece movements apply except special rules for VIP capture.

Good luck and enjoy your game!
